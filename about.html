About

Daily planner was created by Drew Markham, a sophomore at Redwood High School and a member of the class of 2027.

Daily planner was built to streamline the process of planning your day.

Drew often found himself unable to reach his peak productivity due to inneficient or low effort planning leading to procrastination and wasting time.

Daily planner allows for a quick and easy way to create an easy to understand, and optimized schedule to make your day as easy as can be.
